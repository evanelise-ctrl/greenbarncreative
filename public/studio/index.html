Studio

A studio, ten years
in the making.

Greenbarn is a creative studio in Dearborn, Michigan. We make photography, film, and design for brands that would rather look like themselves than like everyone else.

Most of what we know we learned somewhere else first. A decade of brand work across Bedrock, Cisco, the City of Detroit, and a long list of smaller shops taught us how a brand actually gets built and, more usefully, how it falls apart. We started Greenbarn to do that work directly, without the layers in between.

Who you work with

Evan Gonzalez

Photo · Design · Web

Evan spent nearly eight years at Bedrock, finishing as Creative Director, then led the creative team for Cisco's global security brand. She covers photography, print, brand identity, and the web, along with the operations that keep a project on schedule.

Justin

Film · Motion

Justin shoots and cuts. Brand films, event coverage, patient stories, and the kind of short work that has to hold attention in the first two seconds. He has been making video professionally for the better part of a decade, most of it alongside Evan.

About
the barn.

There was a green barn behind a house in Michigan, and two kids who spent every spare hour in it making things. Long before either of them would have called it a career.

Neither of us really stopped. The name is the only thing we took with us, and it still describes the work: two people in a room, building something, getting it right before anyone else sees it.
